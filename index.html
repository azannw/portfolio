About

~/about/README.md

I'm a second-year Computer Science student at FAST. I really care about building communities and connecting with people, whether through writing, conversations, or the podcasts I host.

I love teaching people. At CS Connect Pakistan we do exactly that for free: conversations, podcasts, and sessions with people in tech. We put it all on YouTube so anyone can watch.

I enjoy tinkering with hardware and low level stuff, breaking things, fixing them, and understanding how they work. Beyond tech I like reading about history and religion, and I'm trying to get better at Arabic.

Languages I use to talk to people: Urdu, English, and a bit of Arabic (still on my list to improve). To talk to the computer: C++, Python, and Java.
