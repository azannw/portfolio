About

~/about/README.md

I am a Computer Science student in my 4th semester at FAST, but my story actually started in a medical college.

I was studying for my MBBS when I realized that while I could become a doctor, my heart wasn't in it. I found myself drawn to logic, systems, and how computers work. I made the hard decision to drop out and pursue CS, and honestly, it was the best decision I ever made.

Now, I genuinely enjoy what I do. I founded CS Connect Pakistan in my first semester to bridge the gap between students and the tech industry. While many of my peers worry about grades, I've focused on building real experience, landing corporate jobs, and helping others grow along the way.

I build tools to make my own life easier. When they work well for me, I polish them up and share them with the world. That's how most of my projects happen, organic and useful.

Inspired by Shaykh Atif Ahmed and the idea of aligning work with purpose.
