About

~/about/README.md

I'm a Computer Science student in my 4th semester, but my journey wasn't linear. I realized my passion lay in logic, code, and building things, not in medicine.

I enjoy what I do, and I love what I do :) In my free time, I build stuff that makes my life easier. When I feel it's useful, I make it public. That's how most of my projects start.

I founded CS Connect Pakistan in my first semester with the sole idea of connecting tech students across the country. I've worked in the corporate sector and secured jobs when most peers were just worrying about grades.

Inspired by Shaykh Atif Ahmed.
