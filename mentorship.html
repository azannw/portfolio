If you feel stuck, confused, or overwhelmed with choices in tech, you are not alone.

> my_story.txt

I didn't take the traditional path. I started in MBBS, but I felt misaligned. I felt a pull towards logic, code, and computers that I couldn't ignore. So I took a risk, dropped out, and joined CS at FAST.

It wasn't easy, but it was right. In my first semester, while trying to figure things out myself, I founded CS Connect Pakistan to bring students together. I focused on getting real-world experience, landing corporate jobs, and building a community.

I am still a student, and I don't claim to be a guru. But I have been in the trenches. I have made difficult pivots, managed communities, and helped hundreds of students find clarity.
